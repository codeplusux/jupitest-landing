Jupitest Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04/23/25 Jupitest is an app designed to help entrepreneurs and professionals run moderated tests and validate their ideas with real customers. We take user privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address via Firebase Authentication.
  • App usage data: We collect information about the tests you conduct, including defined tasks, time tracking, and notes, in order to provide usability metrics.
  • Analytical data: We may collect anonymous data about how the app is used (e.g., features accessed) to improve our service.

2. How We Use the Data

  • To authenticate and manage your account.
  • To show you the results of your usability tests and support your idea validation process.
  • To improve the app based on aggregated, anonymous usage patterns.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase by Google (Firestore and Authentication) to manage our database and user authentication. For more details, see Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as you use Jupitest. You may request account and data deletion at any time by contacting us at: ux@pablovivanco.com.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at: ux@pablovivanco.com.
